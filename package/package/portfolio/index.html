3D Printer

I got into 3D printing about a year ago when there was a printer in my summer workplace. At home I introduced myself more deeply to the hobby by googling all kinds of stuff. After a while of preoccupation I decided to buy Ender 3 pro. At the time it was great choice moneywise. Even though in the beginning it took a lot of adjusting, after I finally got it working it has been a great printer ever since. Here I will be posting some of my prints.
